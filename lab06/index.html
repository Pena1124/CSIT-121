Rocky Balboa : A Movie Review

Cast Of The Movie

  • Main Actors: Sylvester Stallone, Talia Shire, Carl Weathers
  • Director: John G. Avildsen
  • Film Location : Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

Movie Facts

  1. Rocky Balboa, a poor up and coming boxer from Philadelphia, gets a shot at the world heavyweight championship.
  2. He trains very hard with the help of his elderly coach, Mickey, and falls in love with Adrian, who then becomes his wife.
  3. Rocky faces the undefeated champion and the best in the world, Apollo Creed, in a very cruel and bloody match. Apollo did not like the fight Rocky put with him because he has never faced off against such a tough and relentless fighter such as Rocky.

Movie Review

Rocky Balboa is an absolute classic which was filmed in 1976. So many years have passed by and I absolutely love watching it over and over and I never get tired of it. Rocky Balboa was the 1st movie of the Rocky series. This unique series made Sylvester Stallone's name known all over the world. This movie gives the feeling of sadness and joy because it tells the story of Rocky as a poor up and coming boxer who was given 1 chance and took advantage of the opportunity and made his name known for being a tough fighter.
