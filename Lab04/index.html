Chicken Quesadillas

Welcome ! You will be learning how to make our special chicken quesadillas ! Recipe By : The Flaming Kitchen Document

Ingredients

Ingredients needed

  • 400g of small chicken cubes
  • Tortillas
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Red Chili Powder and Paprika Powder

Steps

Steps to follow

  1. Put a tortilla on a pan and then put shredded cheese on top
  2. Put the chicken on top of the tortilla
  3. Put another tortilla on top
  4. Heat it up and then enjoy !
