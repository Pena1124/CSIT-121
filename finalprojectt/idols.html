My idols

Welcome ! On this page, I will be talking about the 2 biggest athletes I look up to. I will give their names and why I look up to them. I will also provide a table with some of the best players in history and some of their best accomplishments.

Lionel Messi

My all time favorite soccer player as well as role model is Lionel Messi. He is an Argentinian soccer player who played for FC Barcelona from 2004 to 2021. Growing up, I watched how he played and acted on the field and he was such a great role model to me. He was always a calm and quiet player, but he was always the best on the field. He was never a cocky player and he still remains humble even though he is the best soccer player in the world.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo was a Portoguese soccer player who played for Real Madrid from 2009-2018. In my opinion, he is the 2nd best soccer player in the world. He was Messi's rival for many years. Even though Ronaldo wasn't my favorite player, as I got older, I realized how much I have learned from watching him. He was a cocky player, but he had every right to be because of all the hate and criticism he recieved when he would have bad days.
