Chicken Quesadillas

Recipe by : The Flaming Kitchen

Ingredients Needed

  • 400g of small chicken cubes
  • Tortillas
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Red Chili Powder and Paprika Powder
  • Cumin Powder or Garam Masala
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Salt and Oregano
  • Oil
  • Cut Onions, Chopped Green Peppers, and Chopped Tomatoes

Steps To Follow

  1. Pour the Small Chicken Cubes into a big bowl
  2. Put 1 tsp of red chili powder on the chicken
  3. Put 1/2 tsp of cumin powder or garam masala on the chicken
  4. Put 2 tsp of ginger garlic paste on the chicken
  5. Put 1/2 tsp of paprika powder on the chicken
  6. Put 1/2 tsp of salt on the chicken
  7. Put 1/4 tsp of oregano on the chicken
  8. Put 1 tbsp of oil on the chicken
  9. Mix up the chicken well
  10. Let the chicken sit in the bowl for 15-20 minutes
  11. Put the chicken in a pan and cook on medium flame for 10-12 mins
  12. Put 1/4 cup of diced green peppers on the chicken
  13. Put 1 chopped onion and 1 chopped tomato on the chicken
  14. Mix and cook for 3-5 minutes
  15. Put a tortilla on a pan and then put shredded cheese on top
  16. Put the chicken on top of the tortilla
  17. Put another tortilla on top
  18. Heat it up and then enjoy !

This is one of my favorite recipes because the steps to making it are very simple and it does not take a long time to make. They are also very delicious.
